We released **SEOBNRv6EHM**, a new effective-one-body waveform model that extends the SEOBNR family to
binary black holes on generic planar orbits — bound eccentric orbits, as well as dynamical captures and
scattering encounters — and applied it to the LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA catalog to study the imprint of orbital
eccentricity on the inferred source properties.

The new model is both faster and more accurate than its predecessor, SEOBNRv5EHM, and will serve as the
basis for an upcoming eccentric, precessing model. Stay tuned!

Read the preprints on the model and its data-analysis application:
[arXiv:2605.28715](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28715) and
[arXiv:2605.28716](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28716).
