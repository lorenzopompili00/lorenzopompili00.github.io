In October 2025 I joined the [Nottingham Centre of Gravity](https://www.nottingham.ac.uk/gravity/) at the
[University of Nottingham](https://www.nottingham.ac.uk/) as a Research Fellow, in the
[School of Mathematical Sciences](https://www.nottingham.ac.uk/mathematics/).

There, I work in [Stephen Green](https://stephenrgreen.com/)'s group on [DINGO](https://github.com/dingo-gw/dingo),
an open-source machine-learning framework that uses simulation-based inference — neural posterior estimation — for
rapid and accurate parameter estimation of gravitational-wave signals. I focus on extending and applying these methods,
bringing together my background in waveform modeling with machine-learning approaches to gravitational-wave data analysis.
